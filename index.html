About

A lightweight menstrual-cycle tracker with on-device prediction of your next period. All your data stays in your browser's localStorage — nothing is sent to any server.

The prediction uses a simple rule: next period start = last period start + average cycle length. The fertile window is marked ~14–10 days before the predicted next period.

Backed by an LSTM regressor trained on the mcPHASES menstrual-health research dataset (CV MAE ≈ 4.3 days). See APPROACH.md for the modelling details.
